WakeMeApp

Branding, Graphic design, Mobile design, App design, UX/UI design, Logo design, Illustrator, Sketch

WakeMeApp is an Android application for those who are often late for school, work or anything you should be there before or on time. This app helps you wake up on time and be able to check if your friends are awake or oversleeping.

The idea came from the actual client's experience and they hope for the app to be used among their favorite bubbles!

Design purpose

I designed this app based on client's demand.
What makes client concerned for creating this app was for the app to be effective what it is for and easy to use for someone who just woke up at the same time.
Another thing I thought that is important for the design was for the users to feel home and connected with their bubbles.
