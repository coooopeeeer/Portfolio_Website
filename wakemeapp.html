WakeMeApp

Branding, Product design, User research, UX design, UI design, Visual design, Graphic design, Mobile design, App design, Logo design, Illustrator, Sketch

WakeMeApp is an Android application for those who are often late for school, work or anything you should be there before or on time. This app helps you wake up on time and be able to check if your friends are awake or oversleeping.

The concept comes from actual client's experience and they hope for the app to be used among their favorite bubbles!

Visual Design

Home - Efficient home screen to connect with friends

The home screen shows you a friend list with each status, this makes the user's action easier.

Chat - User-friendly Interface

User-friendly chat room for users to send message with just 1 click

My Page - This is where you set your alarm

You can set your alarm to let your friends know what time you want to wake up so that you'll never need to be worried about oversleeping!

Design purpose

The idea comes from actual client's experience which their friends always came late for the appointment and they found that it's difficult to track their time.
What makes client concerned for creating this app was for the app to be effective for its purpose and easy to use for someone who just woke up at the same time.
Another thing I thought that is important for the design was for the users to feel home and connected with their bubbles.

The goals

  • Install: 2,500 times / 3 months
  • Respond speed: 1 second
  • Help users to enjoy within a private group

Key Challenges

  • For both beginners and advanced users
  • Has log in function with personalized progress charts
  • Design should be customizable by developers
